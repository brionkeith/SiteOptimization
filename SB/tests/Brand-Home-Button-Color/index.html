    OUR SPACIOUS SUITES

    SETTLE IN AND STAY AWHILE

    Settle in and stay a while. Curl up with a good book. A comfy chair makes it easy to wind down before you turn in for the night.  Be productive from your own flexible workspace in the comfort of your suite.  Our fully equipped kitchens have everything you need to cook your favorite meal, whip up a midnight snack or entertain friends.

    THE LONGER YOU STAY, THE LESS YOU PAY

    THE SOCIAL

    Want to unwind after a long day?  Come to the Social every Tuesday–Thursday to relax and get together with other guests over complimentary drinks and conversation starters like sliders, flatbread pizzas, and fresh salads.  

    EXPERIENCE THE MODERN EXTENDED STAY DIFFERENCE

    WELCOMING SPACES

    Every space within our hotel features a special sense of community, comfort and convenience. Get together with a group by the fireplace or the large flatscreen TV. Or just curl up in a quiet nook with a good book. The Patio and The Backyard give you a chance to relax after a long day.
