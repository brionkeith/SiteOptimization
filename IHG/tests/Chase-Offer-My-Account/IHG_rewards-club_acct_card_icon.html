What are Elite Qualifying Points?

What are Elite Qualifying Points?

Elite Qualifying Points are points that count towards your Elite Status qualification. Elite Qualifying Points include points earned for qualifying hotel stays, partner activity, and IHG® Meeting Rewards. To learn more about Elite Qualifying Points, see our Member Terms & Conditions.

What are Elite Rollover Nights?

Elite Rollover Nights are all nights earned last year in excess of 50 - these nights roll over and count towards your re-qualification this year. For more information, please visit our Elite Rollover Nights page.

What are Elite Qualifying Points?

Elite Qualifying Points are points that count towards your Elite Status qualification. Elite Qualifying Points include points earned for qualifying hotel stays, partner activity, and IHG® Meeting Rewards. To learn more about Elite Qualifying Points, see our Member Terms & Conditions.
